See the potential of Deep Learning

In this site you will see how an Artificial Intelligence could accomplish a task starting form zero knowledge, even don't know what are the balls or what's the end

Create your Level

You can place in the map all the possible objects, remeber only that you can place only one player and one end. To change the start direction of a ball you can use the modifier and the show button to see the ball's moving.

Play your Level

You have the possibility of play the level you just created to see if the AI is more smart than you

Let AI plays your Level

In this section you can decide the size of the population, select if you want to see the lines (the eyes of each player), the type of learning (if incremental or not) and than press play to see all the generation :)
